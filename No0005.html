    • Tanomadala Offically Formalizes the Annexation of a New Province in Banten.

    23.06.2026
    Author: Quentin Pieter

    On an earlier article posted on the 19th of June, 2026, it was mentioned that Tanomadala was working on establishing a new province in the city of Anyer, Banten. The planned province was to be establised on a small rock situated near a restaurant called Rumah Makan Katepang 2, near the coast. The claimed rock was very small, only coming up to 2-4m², barley anything compared to anything Tanomadala has previously annexed. Despite the lack of resources and actual benifit, it was still formally integrated into the nation.

    That's right! As of the 23rd of June, 2026, after approval from the Chancellery, the small rock has been officialy integrated into Tanomadala, as an autonomous province. The rock, now named Cibatu, formally the Autonomous Province of Cibatu (Indonesian: Provinsi Otonom Cibatu, Sundanese: Propinsi Otonom Cibatu) is now a part of the republic! The pictures featured above are the region's official flag and emblem, both inspired by the regional emblem of Banten.

    There is currently no formal regional government aside from the national Tanomadalan government, however, the de facto regional autonomous government is run by Rumah Makan Katepang 2, which is the restaurant across the island which was mentioned previously.
