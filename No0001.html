    • Arab Socilalist Union Remains the Only Free Party Inside Tanomadala's Legislature After a Failed Contest

    12.06.2026
    Author: Quentin Pieter

    On the 12th of June, 2026, the Arab Socialist Union (ASU) won every seat inside Tanomadala's Free Party Sector without having to do anything at all. Since their introduction to the legislature on the 7th of June of the same year, Tanomadala has been very open to accepting more free parties to speak inside the legislature, even hosting a "competition" of sorts to start new parties and give voice to people who's ideals may have been supressed by the socialist regime.

    However, even now, as of writing this article, no other parties have been established. Which leads to today. At the end of the "competition," the premise was to have every party be put on a poll on the official Discord server to see which party would be getting representation in the legislature. However, the voting would not follow normal traditional democratic elections, where the one with the most votes would get absolute representation. The premise had it where every vote the party had corresponded with one seat in the legislature to ensure everyone had some form of representation. However, due to the fact that no competition existed, the vote never went through, and since there was only one party (The ASU), all the seats were theirs by default.

    Because of this, the Friday party election has been officially established. Now, every Friday all parties established will be put up against a public vote to see how many seats they will receive inside the legislature. This resolution will hopefully give everyone a chance to have their voices heard and be sure any future parties will never be left out (unless the public wants it so).
