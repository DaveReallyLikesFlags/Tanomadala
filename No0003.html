    • Tanomadala Plans on Annexing a Rock in Banten

    19.06.2026
    Author: Quentin Pieter

    Very recently, president of Tanomadala, Quentin Pieter returned from a short holiday in Anyer, Banten. Although the trip was not for official buisness, he did not come back entirley empty handed. As early as the 17th of June, he had gotten the idea to claim a piece of land, more specifically a rock near the shore, for Tanomadala. The claimed rock lies across a small restaurant called Rumah Makan Katepang 2. The region has not been mapped out but has an estimated 2-4m². The rock is entirley surrounded by water, being the only piece of Tanomadalan land which is not land-locked aside from the nation's Minecraft dependencies.

    As of the 19th of June, 2026, the rock has not been formally or properly incorperated into Tanomadala, and still technically remains unclaimed, however the national legislature is working on changing this fact. The rock is expected to be fully incorperated into the nation around the 20th-25th of June.
