    • Arab Socialist Union Reigns Victorious and Wins Every Seat in the Legislature After Kaiser Lincoln Dissolves the TLP

    21.06.2026
    Author: Quentin Pieter

    On the 19th of June, 2026, the first actual Friday Voting occured, with the Arab Socialist Union going directly against the Tanomadalan League of Patriots. No parties had existed besides the ASU during the previous Friday Voting on the 12th of June, but now existed the TLP, a nationalist movement/party formed by Lincköln or Kaiser Lincoln, which gave the first real competition the ASU had.

    However mid election on the same day, Lincköln mysteriously deleted the original post for the party and left the server. His account was not deleted, as it was still able to be messaged and was still present on other Discord servers. It is speculated that after seeing the voting results, he assumed that it meant his party lost entirley and that they would not get represented whatsoever, so as a result, he deleted the original post, but his reasoning for leaving the server is unknown.

    If Lincköln had stayed, the TLP would have about at least 12 seats in the legislature, however now that they have ceased to exist, all seats have been given back to the ASU by default.

    The poll closed later on the 20th of June, with 75% of the voters in favor of the ASU. That means until now, the Arab Socialist Union remains uncontested and the supreme representative inside the Free Party Sector, or at least for now. On the 21st of June, 2026, it was finally confirmed that the TLP was officialy dissolved and invalid for participation inside the legislature.

    However even thought the ASU still stands strong, even after 2 elections, they might finally face off a worthy opponent during the next election. As right on the 19th, user Izzo started the Tanomadalan Islamic Revolutionaries Party (TIRP), another left-winged party, now representing the Islamic population of the micronation. There is not much information regarding the party, but at least now, maybe the ASU will finally be contested during the next Friday Voting.
