    • Tanomadalan Citizens Have a Duty to Serve the Nation, Says the New Nationalist Movement

    14.06.2026
    Author: Quentin Pieter

    Tanomadala was formerly an unofficial one-party state, entirely ruled by the Tanomadalan Socialist Party. However recently on the 7th of June, 2026, the state, hoping to strengthen democracy and freedom of speech allowed the people to run their own parties and be democratically elected regardless of their political status. The first of these free parties being the Arab Socialist Union, an externally funded party primarily run by the Pharaohate of Rhakotis. The ASU remained the only free party to be established in Tanomadala up until today.

    On the 14th of June, 2026, Lincköln or Kaiser Lincoln, started the Tanomadalan League of Patriots, a patriotic, nationalist movement which is quote "dedicated to the prosperity, unity, and continued growth of the Socialist Democratic Micronation of Tanomadala." The party is heavily dedicated to preservation of Tanomadalan nationalism, patriotism, identity and to ensure a greater future for the people of the nation, and the nation itself.

    The party is the only one with a fully written manifesto. The manifesto lists the following as the party's core principles.

    1. "National Unity Above All"
      The party believes that national unity is the foundation of a strong and stable Tanomadala. They seek to unify the people and create a single Tanomadalan national identity, which does not discriminate or divide the people.
    2. "Patriotism and National Pride"
      The party encourages citizens to take pride in the history, culture, and achievements of the nation. They firmly believe that patriotism inspires people to contribute positivley to the development of the nation and that love for one's country should be demonstrated through service and responsibility. As quoted directly from the manifesto: "A proud people creates a stronger and more respected nation."
    3. "Growth of Citizenship"
      The League supports the peaceful expansion of the Tanomadalan community through the recruitment of dedicated new citizens, believing that the growth of citizenship contributes to the prosperity and influence of Tanomadala.
    4. "Protection of National Identity"
      The manifesto calls the preservation of Tanomadalan culture and tradition a main priority of the party, believing that future generations should inherit a strong sense of national identity and that protecting heritage ensures the continuity of the Tanomadalan spirit.
    5. "International Recognition and Friendship"
      The League seeks peaceful and respectful relations with other micronations and communities and encourages international friendship based on mutual respect and shared interests. Firmly believing that a respected nation can better represent the interests of its people.
    6. "Service to the Nation"
      The party strongly encourages every citizen bear responsibility to contribute to the development of the Tanomadalan state. Calling the contribution an honor and a duty. The party encourages contribution through public administration, cultural work, diplomacy, and community projects, believing that dedication and hard work strengthen national unity.

    The party has not yet entered the legislature, however due to its strong nationalism it is predicted to have a majority vote, even standing above the ASU.
